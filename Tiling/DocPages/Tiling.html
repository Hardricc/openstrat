Tiling Module

Contents

  • Abstract regular tile geometry.
  • Square and hex tile grid geometry.
  • OfTile classes for the display of tiles.

Turn Resolution

  • Verify directives from Players and set them as intentions
  • Aggregate Intentions.
  • Resolve Segment
    1. Accumulate. Aggregate the intentions towards each tile for each tile.
    2. Ajudicate. Determine the outcome of the intentions and processes.
    3. Distribute. Distribute the effects upon the new state for the effected tiles.
  • Repeat Resolve Segement till the given number of segments has been repeated.
  • Inform the players, human and AI, of the Turn resolution.
